**Uczenie Głębokie** (Deep Learning) is an engineering course at Wrocław University of Science and Technology, delivered across 30 hours of lectures and 15 practical sessions (22.5 hours total). The course systematically builds from fundamentals to advanced topics in deep learning, with an emphasis on both theory and hands-on implementation.

## What it is

The course is structured in four lecture blocks and six lab blocks, built on a Hugo site with automated GitHub Pages deployment:

- **Lecture Blocks**: Foundations of neural networks · Convolutional networks and generative models · Sequence models, attention, and Transformers · Advanced topics (deep RL, interpretability, segmentation)
- **Lab Blocks**: NumPy and PyTorch fundamentals · CNNs with regularization and transfer learning · Generative models (autoencoders, VAE, GAN) · RNNs, LSTM, attention mechanisms, Transformers · Deep reinforcement learning · Explainability and adversarial robustness

## My role

I designed and delivered the complete curriculum, including:

- Lecture material spanning backpropagation, regularization, and modern architectures (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers)
- Hands-on labs progressing from NumPy matrix operations to production practices (experiment tracking, reproducibility)
- Integration of interpretability (Grad-CAM, SHAP, LIME) and security (adversarial attacks) into the practical component
- Course infrastructure: Hugo-based site with automated CI/CD via GitHub Actions
